## Benchmark by stage

As fleets scale, utilization usually drops before systems catch up. That is normal, but unmanaged drops become expensive quickly.

## Use this benchmark logic

- 1-5 vehicles: manual responsiveness can keep utilization high
- 6-20 vehicles: scheduling and maintenance coordination become core constraints
- 20+ vehicles: communication and process variability are biggest risks

## Downtime breakdown matters

Split downtime into:

- Maintenance delay
- Turnover lag
- Demand gap
- Platform listing issues

Once categorized, improvement plans become obvious.

## Build your utilization review

Run these questions weekly:

1. Which vehicles are below target?
2. Is the issue demand, readiness, or pricing?
3. What one process change should happen this week?

## Final takeaway

Utilization is a systems KPI. If you benchmark correctly and respond quickly, scaling gets easier and more predictable.
