## The core difference

Manual pricing depends on human observation and available time. AI pricing depends on continuous signal processing and predefined guardrails.

## Tradeoffs table

| Approach | Strength | Constraint |
|---|---|---|
| Manual | Flexible judgment | Slow and inconsistent at scale |
| AI | Fast signal response | Requires setup and monitoring |
| Hybrid | Balanced transition | Needs clear ownership model |

## Best rollout path

1. Select one segment for pilot rollout.
2. Set floor and ceiling constraints.
3. Run two to four weeks of comparison.
4. Expand only when KPI lift is consistent.

## KPI stack for fair comparison

- Conversion rate
- Revenue per available day
- Utilization
- Average lead time to booking

Without a full KPI view, pricing decisions become biased.

## Final takeaway

AI pricing is not a magic switch. It is an operating capability that becomes powerful when paired with process discipline.
