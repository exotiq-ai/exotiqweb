## Where spreadsheets still work

Spreadsheets are useful for:

- Early-stage tracking
- Ad hoc analysis
- Simple one-person operations

The challenge starts when multiple workflows and people need consistent coordination.

## Common spreadsheet failure points

- Version conflicts and stale data
- Manual status updates
- Missed maintenance and turnover tasks
- Slow response cycles for pricing changes

These issues compound with each new vehicle.

## Migration framework

1. Identify top three operational pain points.
2. Replace one workflow at a time.
3. Train team members with clear SOP ownership.
4. Validate KPI improvements before full cutover.

## Final takeaway

The right time to migrate is before process friction starts harming guest experience and fleet economics.
