# Exotiq.ai - AI-Powered Fleet Management Platform

## Company Overview
Exotiq.ai is an AI-powered fleet management platform designed for exotic car rental operators and Turo hosts. Built by operators who scaled to 15+ vehicles, the platform automates pricing, maintenance, guest communications, and operations.

## Core Value Proposition
Turn wasted hours into revenue-generating time. Automate pricing, availability, maintenance, and guest messaging with AI built for fleet operators.
