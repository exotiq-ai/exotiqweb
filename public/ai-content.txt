# exotiq.ai - AI-Powered Fleet Management Platform

## Company Overview
exotiq.ai is an AI-powered fleet management platform designed for exotic car rental operators. Built by operators who scaled a professional fleet (originally migrating from Turo), the platform automates pricing, maintenance, guest communications, and operations.
