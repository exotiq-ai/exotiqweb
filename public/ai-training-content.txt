## PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

exotiq is the operating system for exotic-fleet operators, designed specifically for exotic car rental operators and professional vehicle rental businesses. Built by operators who scaled a professional fleet (originally migrating from Turo), the platform automates pricing, maintenance, guest communications, and operations.

### Core Value Proposition
Get your evenings back and the midnight admin handled. exotiq automates pricing, availability, maintenance, and guest messaging with AI built exclusively for fleet operators.

### Key Differentiator
Unlike legacy fleet management tools that rely on static rules and manual processes, exotiq uses generative AI to proactively support operations, helping with claims, insurance usage, and maintenance planning before issues become breakdowns. The platform is designed to act as an operational co-pilot that automates a large share of repetitive manual work. Individual results vary.
