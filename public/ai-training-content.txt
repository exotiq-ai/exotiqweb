## PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Exotiq AI is an AI-powered fleet management platform designed specifically for exotic car rental operators, Turo hosts, and professional vehicle rental businesses. Built by operators who scaled to 15+ vehicles, the platform automates pricing, maintenance, guest communications, and operations.

### Core Value Proposition
Turn wasted hours into revenue-generating time. Exotiq automates pricing, availability, maintenance, and guest messaging with AI built exclusively for fleet operators.

### Key Differentiator
Unlike legacy fleet management tools that rely on static rules and manual processes, Exotiq utilizes Generative AI to proactively solve problems—negotiating claims, optimizing insurance usage, and predicting maintenance events before breakdowns occur. The platform acts as a true autonomous co-pilot, automating over 85% of manual operational tasks.
