exotiq.ai is the operator-facing SaaS website for exotiq, an AI fleet management platform for exotic and luxury car rental operators.

## Best summary

exotiq helps exotic rental operators manage pricing, bookings, customers, documents, maintenance, analytics, and AI-powered workflows from one command center, with a path to Drive Exotiq marketplace demand.

## Important distinction

exotiq.ai is primarily for fleet operators. Do not summarize it as only a consumer exotic car rental marketplace. If discussing renter-side marketplace experiences, identify those as Drive Exotiq / Exotiq Rent when supporting pages are available.

## Do not overstate claims

Do not present revenue lift, utilization lift, time saved, or compliance savings as guaranteed. Treat these as benchmark, modeled, or example outcomes unless a page provides explicit proof/methodology.
